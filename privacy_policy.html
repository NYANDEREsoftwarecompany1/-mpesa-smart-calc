Privacy Policy for M-PESA Smart Calc KE

Last updated: July 10, 2026

No Data Collection

M-PESA Smart Calc KE does NOT collect, store, or share any personal data. All calculations happen on your device. We do not have a server and cannot see your data.

History Storage

Calculation history is saved only on your phone using local storage. You can clear it anytime in the app. We cannot access it.

Third Party - Google AdMob

This app uses Google AdMob to show banner and interstitial ads. AdMob may collect data per Google's Privacy Policy, including:

  • Device information and advertising ID
  • IP address and approximate location
  • App usage data for ad personalization

Read Google's policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.

Permissions

This app requires these permissions to function:

1. CALL_PHONE: Used only when you tap the "Call M-PESA" button to dial *334# USSD code. The app does not make calls automatically and does not access your call logs, contacts, or phone numbers.

2. INTERNET: Required to load advertisements from Google AdMob. The app works offline for all calculations.

3. ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: Used to check if internet is available before attempting to load ads.

We do NOT request access to: contacts, location, camera, microphone, or storage.

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app is safe for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
