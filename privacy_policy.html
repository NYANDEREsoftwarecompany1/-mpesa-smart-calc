Privacy Policy for M-PESA Smart Calc KE

Last Updated: July 6, 2026

Developer: NYANDERE Software Company

1. Data We Collect

We collect ZERO personal data.

  • We do NOT collect names, phone numbers, or M-PESA PINs
  • We do NOT store your calculation history on our servers
  • We do NOT access your M-PESA account or transactions
  • We do NOT track who you send money to
  • We do NOT require account creation or login

2. Data Stored on Your Device

Your calculation history is saved ONLY on your phone using local storage. This data never leaves your device. You can delete it anytime by clearing app data in Settings. We cannot access it.

3. Third-Party Services

This app uses these services:

  • Google AdMob: Displays ads to keep the app free. Google may collect anonymous advertising identifiers. See Google Privacy Policy.
  • Phone Dialer: When you tap "DIAL *334#", we open your phone app. We do not see who you call or record calls.
  • WhatsApp: When you share, text is sent via WhatsApp. We do not access your contacts.

4. App Permissions

  • CALL_PHONE: Used ONLY to open dialer with *334# pre-filled. We never make calls automatically.
  • INTERNET: Used ONLY to load ads. All fee calculations work 100% offline.

6. Children's Privacy

This app is intended for users aged 13+. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

7. Your Rights

Since we collect no personal data, there is nothing to request or delete. To remove local history, uninstall the app or clear app data.
